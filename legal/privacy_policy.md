# Privacy Policy for EchoMind AI
**Last Updated:** January 17, 2026

## 1. Introduction
EchoMind AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children, students, and parents. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard personal information in compliance with the **Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)** and other applicable laws.

## 2. Information We Collect
### 2.1 Information from Children (Under 13)
In compliance with COPPA, we verify parental consent before collecting any personal information from children under 13.
- **Username**: We encourage pseudonyms (e.g., "SpaceExplorer22").
- **Learning Data**: Progress in Knowledge Tree, quizzes attempts, and educational interactions.
- **Chat Logs**: Interactions with the AI tutor (filtered for PII).

### 2.2 Information from Parents
- **Account Details**: Email address, encrypted password.
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely via third-party providers (Stripe/Apple/Google).

## 3. How We Use Information
- To provide personalized Socratic tutoring.
- To track progress and generate parent reports.
- To detect and prevent unsafe content using our Safety System.
- **WE DO NOT SELL DATA TO ADVERTISERS.**

## 4. Safety & PII Protection
- All chat interaction is monitored by automated safety filters.
- Personally Identifiable Information (PII) like phone numbers or addresses is automatically redacted from our logs.
- We employ "Prompt Injection Defense" to ensure the AI remains in a safe, educational mode.

## 5. Data Security
We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit.

## 6. Parental Rights
Parents have the right to:
- Review their child's personal information.
- Request deletion of their child's account.
- Refuse further collection of data.
