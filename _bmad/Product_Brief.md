# Product Brief: EchoMind

## Overview
EchoMind is an AI-powered mental wellness platform that combines Socratic questioning methodology with gamification to provide personalized mental health support and self-reflection tools.

## Vision
To create an accessible, engaging, and effective mental wellness companion that helps users develop deeper self-awareness and emotional intelligence through guided introspection.

## Target Audience
- Individuals seeking mental wellness support
- People interested in self-reflection and personal growth
- Users looking for accessible mental health resources
- Those who prefer guided, conversational approaches to therapy

## Key Features
1. **Socratic Chat Engine**: AI-driven conversational interface using Socratic questioning
2. **Gamification System**: Points, badges, and achievements to encourage engagement
3. **Tree Visualization**: Visual representation of thought patterns and progress
4. **Safety & Moderation**: Content filtering and crisis detection
5. **User Progress Tracking**: Analytics and insights into mental wellness journey

## Success Metrics
- User engagement and retention rates
- Session completion rates
- User satisfaction scores
- Mental wellness improvement indicators

## Competitive Advantages
- Unique combination of Socratic methodology and gamification
- Visual thought pattern mapping
- Comprehensive safety features
- Personalized AI-driven conversations
